User guide

Project Track Customize Log Entries Customize Invoice Save Invoice

1. Add new project

  • On the "Projects" page, click on "Add new project"
  • Fill out form with project details
  • Client: you will be presented with a drop-down choice of clients. If it's your first time, you will be asked to add a new client before proceeding
  • Name: call your project any name you'd like - this will not show up on your invoice
  • Hourly rate: enter the salary you receive per hour
  • Currency: select the currency of your hourly rate, as well as the currency you will invoice in. If these differ, currency conversion features will be turned on automatically

2. Track time

  • On the "Projects" page, click on "Track" next to the relevant project
  • When you start working, click on 'Start tracking your time' button
  • When you have finished working, click on the 'Stop tracking your time' button
  • Hint: You can fully customize each log entry, so don't worry if you forget to stop the timer. You can change its name (which will display on your invoice), date and length or delete it altogether
  • Subtotal: your salary will be calculated automatically based on the time you spent working

3. Invoice

  • On the "Invoices" page, click on "Add new invoice" and select your desired project
  • Click the 'Edit and print' button next to your new invoice
  • Customize: you will be able to customize your invoice before saving it as a PDF
  • Features: you can add your saved billing details, change the invoice number and customise the final amount by adding an additional charge, discount or VAT
